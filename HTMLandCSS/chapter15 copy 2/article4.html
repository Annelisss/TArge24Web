Touring Diary: A sketchbook in your basket

20 MARCH 2025

As you pedal along quiet country roads
and bustling city streets, the
sketchbook in your basket becomes more
than just a collection of pages—it turns
into a living record of your journey.
Each stop offers something new to capture,
whether it's the golden glow of a sunrise
over rolling hills, the charming façade
of a hidden café, or the intricate
atterns of an old cobblestone bridge.
