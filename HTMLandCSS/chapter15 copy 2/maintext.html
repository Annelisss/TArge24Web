Fixed Gear Forever

16 APRILL 2025

The veloheld combines minimalist
design with superb quality. Devoid
of excessive graphics and gear shift
components, the veloheld product
range delights us with its beauty and
simplicity. The black and white
(ying and yang?) bicycles feature
a short wheelbase, a single gear and
narrow handlebar... All you need to
explore the city streets.

For those who want to create their
bike themselves, the veloheld frames
come in three sizes and two
colors and are the perfect
starting point. You can then customize
your ride with a selection of
high-quality components, ensuring a
perfect fit for your riding style.
Each frame is made from lightweight
yet durable materials, offering a
smooth and responsive cycling experience.
