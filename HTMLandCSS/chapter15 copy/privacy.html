1. WHO WE ARE

Bike Haven is dedicated to providing high-quality bicycles, accessories, and rental services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal data.

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number (if provided for orders or inquiries).
  • Purchase & Rental Data: Orders, rental bookings, payment history.
  • Usage Data: Site interactions, preferences, and feedback.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To process orders and manage bike rentals.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To send updates, offers, and relevant cycling news (only if you opt-in).
  • To analyze website usage and improve our services.

4. DATA SECURITY

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. THIRD-PARTY SHARING

We only share necessary data with payment providers (e.g., PayPal, Stripe) and delivery partners to complete your orders. We do not sell or trade your personal information.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You can request access, modification, or deletion of your data. Contact us at support@bikehaven.com.

7. COOKIES

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

8. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at support@bikehaven.com.
